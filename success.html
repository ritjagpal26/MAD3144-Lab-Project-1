Habbits

“We are what we repeatedly do. Excellence, then, is not an act, but a habit.”

Habits can MAKE you, but they can also BREAK you. One of the key differences between the successful & unsuccessful, between those at the highest level, and those destined for the common life, is HABITS. Develop strong daily habits:

  1. SET GOALS.
  2. MAKE A CLEAR PLAN.
  3. EXERCISE.
  4. READ MORE, LEARN MORE.

Every new strong habit you make will plant a seed of success in your future. All these little seeds will grow over time. EVERY ACTION MATTERS, every single day.

4 Steps to Transforming Yourself From Who You Are to Who You Want to Be
See yourself outside yourself. Imagine you are a sculptor.
just do it what you want to do.
Practice every day, no matter what. Change is not something you do some days and then take a break from other days.
Change is a shift in lifestyle.
Set realistic goals. You can't just wake up one morning and say, "I'm not going to be impatient anymore!"
Focus on that for a few weeks, and then go from there..
You have to take risks. You will never become the person you want to be by continuing to be the person you currently are.
Unless you are willing to take that risk, you will forever stay exactly where you are.
